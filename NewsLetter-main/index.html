MOULANA COLLEGE

NEWS LETTER


Learning Begins With Us: We, at Moulana college of arts, science & commere, chennara offer supportive and inspirational environments for young enquiring minds to learn and grow with us. Our passion for learning means we achieve more than outstanding results. We strive to build confident and creative thinkers and aim at delivering an education that is truly relevant to their future.

MANAGER: MUHAMMED YASEEN.P.P



Every human being is precious in the sight of God and to their own parents. Each one have their own individuality, special skills and talents. Moulana College provides an appropriate, all encompassing education, which leads to all round personal development of a person to face the challenges of life in all their future endeavor. Students at our Moulana College are provided with genial tranquil and natural learning environment. We ensure that students entrusted to us grow to their full potential while constantly being groomed to pass out as able one to take responsibility in all walks of life.

I believe that right approach to motivate and get the best out of the students is based on the positive reinforcement of good work and good behaviour with a philosophy which centers on praise, encouragement, enthusiasm,fun and affection rather than criticism,fear and punishment.Education is not accumulation of knowledge like an encyclopedia or the storage bank of a computer.Education should avoid being accumulative and be purely trans formative.The benefits of education are manifold.My dear students excellence is based on knowledge,training,careful study, confidence and competence. They are the factors that give you the courage to act.


ADMINISTRATOR: MUHAMMED RASI.K



Education at Moulana college goes like beyond the acquisition of facts from the books of instruction in a lecture hall.We have always strive to enrich the lives of every member of this wonderful institution. Realizing the philosophy we at noble college commit to foster and save the essence of education building character appreciating values and right attitude acquiring life skills respecting fellow beings and the environments accept differences and building lasting friendship all this by ever expanding the learning horizons and life experiences.


PRINCIPAL:DR.SATHEESH.K.P



As the adage goes, education occupies the prominent place in the evolution and development of any civilized society. Right from the days of Nalanda and takshashila, India has been a country where wisdom is given pivotal importance by the rulers. Taking clue from such historical institutions, today Moulana College of Arts Science and Commerce, Chennara P.O., Tirur, Malappuram, Kerala, has been spearheading the cause of higher education to the socially, economically weaker sections of the society..

It is situated in a coastal village and socially and economically backward region of Kerala. This college since its installation has been doing yeomen service to the society by providing quality education to the learners who are almost first generation graduates. Within a short span of time, this institution has already created a niche for itself in the academic milieu of this part of the district Under the dynamic and visionary management, the college administration under by principal ship is delivering the goods to the stakeholders. I am glad that the institution is growing in all spheres. And I assure that the college will be thriving for creating a knowledge society in the days to come. The college continues its journey crossing several milestones, carrying out its mission and realizing its vision.
